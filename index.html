18:00 — 18:35
迎賓與專屬合影
於門口迎接親友並直接合照留念，人數不多，時間充裕可從容拍攝高畫質合影。
18:35 — 18:45
晚宴開場
請大家入座，燈光調暗播放開場影片，凝聚全場注意力。
18:45 — 19:05
誓詞與溫馨時刻
新人進場後互相發表誓詞，並邀請雙方家長致詞，留下最重要的感動畫面。
19:05 — 19:15
甜蜜儀式
進行切蛋糕儀式，隨後帶領全場舉杯（Toast），宣布晚宴正式開始，請大家盡情享用美食！
19:15 — 21:30
晚宴開席與活動穿插
19:15 準時出第一道菜，開席期間穿插以下活動：
20:15 — 20:30 友人致辭
開席約一小時後，氣氛最放鬆，邀請好友上台分享趣事。
20:50 — 21:30 抽獎與互動遊戲
趁上甜點、水果與咖啡時進行，邊吃甜點邊玩遊戲，將晚宴帶向高潮。
21:30 — 22:00
自由交流與送客
活動與餐點皆已盡興，保留最後 30 分鐘讓親友自由走動聊天，接著發放送客小禮物，晚上 10:00 準時、優雅地圓滿落幕。
18:00 — 18:35
Guest Arrival & Photo Session
Welcome guests at the entrance and take photos right away — with an intimate guest list, 35 minutes is plenty for high-quality group shots.
18:35 — 18:45
Banquet Opening
Guests take their seats as the lights dim for the opening video, drawing everyone's attention.
18:45 — 19:05
Vows & Heartfelt Moments
After the grand entrance, the couple exchanges vows and invites both sets of parents to speak — the night's most touching moments.
19:05 — 19:15
Sweet Ceremony
Cake-cutting ceremony, followed by a toast led by the couple: "Dinner is officially served — enjoy!"
19:15 — 21:30
Dinner Service & Interleaved Activities
The first course is served promptly at 19:15, with activities woven throughout dinner:
20:15 — 20:30 Friends' Speeches
About an hour in, when guests are most relaxed, friends take the stage to share stories.
20:50 — 21:30 Raffle & Interactive Games
Timed around dessert, fruit, and coffee — playing games while eating dessert brings the evening to a joyful climax.
21:30 — 22:00
Mingle & Farewell
With activities and dining wrapped up, the last 30 minutes are for guests to mingle freely. Favors are handed out as the evening wraps up elegantly, right on time at 10:00 PM.
